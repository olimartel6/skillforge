Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How does Skilly work?

Skilly gives you a personalized 5-minute daily challenge based on your chosen skill. Complete the challenge, record your practice, and receive AI-powered feedback to improve. Track your progress through the skill tree and earn badges along the way.

Skills

What skills can I learn?

We offer 30+ skills across multiple categories including Drawing, Guitar, Piano, Magic Tricks, Trading, Business, Cooking, Photography, Coding, Dance, and many more. You can switch between skills at any time without losing progress.

Skills

Can I switch skills without losing progress?

Yes! Your progress is saved separately for each skill. When you switch to a new skill, your previous skill's progress (streaks, roadmap, badges, mini-game levels) is preserved. Switch back anytime to continue where you left off.

Mini-Games

What are mini-games?

Mini-games are interactive quiz-style activities inspired by Duolingo. They include 12 different game types: multiple choice, true/false, match pairs, speed sort, memory cards, and more. Each lesson focuses on a specific topic within your skill. Mini-games are a Premium feature.

Premium

What's included in Premium?

Premium ($6.99/month) unlocks:

  • Unlimited skills (Free is limited to 1 skill)
  • All 12 mini-game types with 24 lessons per skill
  • Advanced AI feedback with detailed analysis
  • Community features (share & interact with other learners)
  • Streak freeze tokens (2/month)
  • XP system with levels and combos
Premium

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel:

  • Open iPhone Settings
  • Tap your name at the top
  • Tap Subscriptions
  • Find Skilly and tap Cancel Subscription
Streaks

How do streaks work?

Complete at least one practice session per day to maintain your streak. If you miss a day, a freeze token is automatically used to preserve your streak (Premium users get 2 tokens/month). Missing 2+ consecutive days resets your streak.

AI Feedback

How does AI feedback work?

After completing a practice session, take a photo or video of your work. Our AI coach (powered by Claude) analyzes your practice and provides specific feedback on your strengths, areas to improve, and a pro tip for your next session.

Account

How do I delete my data?

To delete all your data, go to Profile → Settings → Sign Out. This removes all locally stored data including your profile, progress, and preferences. For any data stored on our servers, email us at support@skillyapp.com with your deletion request.
