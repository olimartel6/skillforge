Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

SkillForge ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Name and date of birth (during onboarding)
  • Skill preferences and learning goals
  • Practice session data (photos, videos recorded during practice)

Information collected automatically:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage data (features used, session duration, streak data)
  • Crash reports and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize your learning experience
  • To generate AI-powered feedback on your practice sessions
  • To track your progress, streaks, and achievements
  • To send practice reminders (with your permission)
  • To improve our app and develop new features

3. AI Processing

When you submit practice photos or videos, they are sent to our AI service (powered by Anthropic's Claude) for analysis. The AI provides feedback on your technique. Media is processed in real-time and is not stored permanently on our servers after analysis is complete.

4. Data Storage

Most of your personal data (name, preferences, progress, streaks) is stored locally on your device. Skill catalog data is hosted on Supabase servers in the United States. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Database and storage infrastructure
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI-powered practice analysis
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and payment processing
  • Apple App Store — App distribution and in-app purchases

6. Subscriptions & Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information directly. All billing is handled by Apple according to their terms and privacy policy.

7. Children's Privacy

SkillForge is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of notifications
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
